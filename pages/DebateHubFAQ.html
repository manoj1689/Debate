Getting Started

How do I create my first debate?

To start your first debate:

  1. Click the "New Debate" button in your dashboard
  2. Choose a debate format (Beginner, Advanced, or Custom)
  3. Set your topic and position
  4. Select AI opponent difficulty
  5. Begin your debate session

What debate formats are available?

DebateHub offers several debate formats:

  • Beginner AI Debate - Perfect for newcomers
  • Advanced AI Debate - For experienced debaters
  • Custom Format - Design your own debate structure
  • Tournament Mode - Compete with other users
  • Practice Mode - Focus on specific skills
