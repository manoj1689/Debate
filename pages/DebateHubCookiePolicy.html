What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences, analyzing site usage, and assisting with our marketing efforts.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

Always enabled

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Optional

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your online activity to help advertisers deliver more relevant advertising or to limit how many times you see an ad.

Optional

How to Control Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. Here's how to do it in your browser:

Google Chrome
  1. Click the menu icon in the browser's toolbar
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click "Privacy and security"
  4. Choose "Cookies and other site data"
  5. Adjust your cookie settings as needed
Mozilla Firefox
  1. Click the menu button
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click "Privacy & Security"
  4. Navigate to "Cookies and Site Data"
  5. Adjust your cookie settings as needed
Safari
  1. Click "Safari" in the menu bar
  2. Choose "Preferences"
  3. Click "Privacy"
  4. Adjust your cookie settings as needed
